About us

Slammers Beach Volleyball is a family run business, locally owned and operated for the last 25 years. We pride ourselves on customer service excellence, and our competition grade Beach Volleyball courts are second to none in North Queensland.

Slammers Beach Volleyball boasts first class onsite amenities including:

  • Competition grade courts
  • Deluxe changerooms
  • Slammers Canteen
    • Drinks, Snacks, Sandwiches and the Best burgers in Town
  • Slammers Licenced bar
    • Happy hour every day from 6-7pm
  • Free onsite WiFi*

* speak to our friendly staff for details.
