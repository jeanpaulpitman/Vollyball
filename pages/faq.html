Fun Volleyball Fact!

There are 800 million players worldwide who play volleyball at least once a week. That's 33 x the population of Australia playing Volleyball each week!

Low or High impact?

Volleyball is a sport that is safe, fun and does not require extreme fitness.Since you don’t have to move around the court too much, it’s great for people who want a low-impact sport that will help them get fit without pushing them to their limit.

Is it competitive?

For those who are a bit more competitive, the sport is fantastic because games can be really exciting and intense, but there’s always a net between you and your opponents

Who can play Beach volleyball?

Beach volleyball is suitable for people of all ages, and men and women alike.

Do I need a medical certificate to play?

No, however, anyone considering starting a new sport or exercise program should see their doctor first.
