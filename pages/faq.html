FAQ

Welcome, We have compiled a list of Frequently Asked Questions for your convience. Got a rules question? check out the Game Rules here!.

Fun Fact! 800 million players globally

There are 800 million players worldwide who play volleyball at least once a week. That's 33x Australia's population playing Volleyball each week!

Is Beach Volleyball Low or High impact sport?

Volleyball is a sport that is safe, fun and does not require extreme fitness.Since you don’t have to move around the court too much, it’s great for people who want a low-impact sport that will help them get fit without pushing them to their limit.

Is it a contact sport?

No. However, for those who are a bit more competitive, the sport is fantastic because games can be really exciting and intense, but there’s always a net between you and your opponents

Who can play Beach Volleyball?

Beach volleyball is suitable for people of all ages, men and women alike. There are 800 million players worldwide who play volleyball at least once a week.

Do I need a medical certificate to play?

No you do not need a medical certificate to play. However, anyone considering starting a new sport or exercise program should see their doctor first.
